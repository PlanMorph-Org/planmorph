## 5. Payments and Financial Terms

### 5.1 Design Pricing

- Architects set the price for their designs in Kenya Shillings (KES)
- Prices are displayed in multiple currencies (KES, USD, EUR, GBP, UGX, TZS, ZAR) with conversion rates
- All payments are processed in KES through Paystack
- Prices are inclusive of the platform commission

### 5.2 Payment Processing

**Payment Methods:**
- **Primary:** Paystack (credit/debit cards, bank transfers, mobile money)
- **Future Support:** M-Pesa, direct bank transfers, additional gateways

**Payment Process:**
1. Client selects design and creates order
2. Order is generated with unique order number (format: PM-YYYYMMDD-XXXXX)
3. Client is redirected to Paystack for secure payment
4. Payment is verified via webhook and callback verification
5. Order status changes from "Pending" to "Paid"
6. Client receives order confirmation email
7. Design files become available for download

### 5.3 Commission Structure

**Design Sales (Architects & Engineers):**
- Architects receive **70% of design sale price**
- PlanMorph retains **30% platform commission**
- Example: KES 50,000 design = KES 35,000 to architect, KES 15,000 to PlanMorph

**Construction Contracts (Contractors):**
- PlanMorph charges **2% commission on estimated construction costs**
- Example: KES 5,000,000 construction project = KES 100,000 commission
- Contractor receives remaining amount from client (less any direct costs)
- Commission is calculated on the estimated construction cost provided by the architect

### 5.4 Payment Schedule

- Design commissions are paid to architects monthly
- Minimum payout threshold: KES 5,000
- Payments are processed within 7 business days of month-end
- Architects must provide valid bank account or mobile money details
- Payment statements are available in the architect dashboard

### 5.5 Refund Policy
