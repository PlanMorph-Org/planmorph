# PlanMorph Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 11, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to PlanMorph ("we," "our," or "us"). PlanMorph operates an online marketplace connecting architects and engineers with clients seeking architectural designs and construction services. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at planmorph.software (the "Platform"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Platform.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 With Service Providers

**Paystack (Payment Processing):**
- We use Paystack to process payments securely
- Paystack receives transaction information including email, amount, and payment reference
- Paystack is PCI-DSS compliant and handles all payment card data
- We never store credit card numbers on our servers
- Paystack Privacy Policy: https://paystack.com/privacy

**DigitalOcean Spaces (File Storage):**
- Design files and professional documents are stored on DigitalOcean Spaces (S3-compatible storage)
- Files are organized in private buckets with access controls
- Download URLs are time-limited and cryptographically signed (60-minute expiration)
- DigitalOcean Privacy Policy: https://www.digitalocean.com/legal/privacy-policy

**Email Service Providers (SMTP/SendGrid):**
- We use email service providers to send transactional emails
- Email providers process your email address and name for delivery
- Emails include order confirmations, account notifications, and password resets

### 4.2 With Other Platform Users

**Architect and Engineer Profiles:**
- Professional names may be displayed with their designs
- Portfolio information may be visible to potential clients

**Order Information:**
- Design titles and architect information are shared with purchasing clients
- Client location information is shared with assigned contractors (for construction services only)

### 4.3 For Legal and Safety Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:
- Valid legal requests from government authorities

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption:** Passwords are hashed using ASP.NET Identity's secure hashing algorithms
- **HTTPS/TLS:** All data transmission is encrypted in transit using TLS
- **JWT Authentication:** Secure, stateless authentication using JSON Web Tokens (60-minute expiration)
- **Signed URLs:** File download links are cryptographically signed with limited validity
- **Access Controls:** Role-based authorization restricts data access
- **Database Security:** PostgreSQL database with secure connection strings
- **API Security:** HMAC-SHA512 signature validation for payment webhooks

### 5.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Limited employee access to personal information
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Incident response procedures
- Secure development practices
- Staff training on data protection

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Soft Deletes:** Most records are soft-deleted (marked as deleted but retained)
- **Professional Review Logs:** Maintain approval/rejection audit trails
- **Transaction Records:** Retained for accounting and compliance purposes
- **Deleted Accounts:** Account data may be retained for legal and operational reasons

**Despite our efforts, no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.**
